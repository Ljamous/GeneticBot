Interpretation. 
The risk models (Tyrer-Cuzick, BOADICEA/CanRisk, BRCAPRO) answer a different question (how much risk?) than GeneticBot (does this person meet NCCN testing criteria, and why?). No listed tool combines guideline-grounded eligibility rationale, integrated pedigree capture, and an education/consent workflow in a single locally deployable package. We make no priority ('first') claim; the novelty is this integration.
